The beginning of this company started with simple idea to deal with a vexation that almost every gamer has faced. This simple yet powerful idea led this company to what it is today. In this section of the website,I will guide you through how this company was built.

Before I started this company, I was a professional gamer. That required me to spend my time religiously in improving my skills and practicing my strategies. Being a gamer, I had to travel different places to attend workshops and tournaments.

During my stay at those places, I had very little time to practice as most of my time was consumed to download and set up the games on the system. With this grievance in my mind, I went on to launch this product.

The product is a high speed mini drive,like a pendrive, that has a game preinstalled in it by the developer. Gamer Pizza's security protocols ensure pirating of games is at a minimum.

This little pendrive holds your own setup thats is fully customizable. We go a step further to make it cross platform compatible. All of your settings including the OS specific settings can be replicated on any system using our product.

Now, all thats left to do is to plug that drive into any PC.GAME ON!
